About CKPCET

Chhotubhai Gopalbhai Patel Institute of Technology (CKPCET), located in the vibrant city of Surat, is one of Gujarat's leading engineering institutions. Since its establishment, CKPCET has been committed to providing top-quality education and fostering innovation in the fields of engineering, technology, and applied sciences.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that nurtures technically sound professionals who contribute to the development of society and the nation.

Our Mission

  • Provide a dynamic learning environment that empowers students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge.
  • Foster innovation, research, and development to address real-world challenges.
  • Promote collaboration with industries and other institutions to enhance the student learning experience.
  • Encourage holistic development by nurturing leadership, ethics, and responsibility in every student.

Why Choose CKPCET?

At CKPCET, we offer an inspiring environment equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on research and development. Our campus culture promotes curiosity, creativity, and collaboration, ensuring that students are well-prepared for both professional careers and higher studies.

Our Values

  • Excellence in Academics
  • Innovation and Research
  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Community and Inclusiveness
  • Sustainability and Social Responsibility
